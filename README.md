# LuwakX

Discover Luwak, the transformative tool by ZENTA. A critical component of the revolutionary Aistronaut, Luwak is a catalyst for change, creating structure from the unstructured in the realm of medical data.

Harnessing the power of advanced large language models, Luwak skillfully converts unstructured data - from medical images, reports and EHRs - into structured data. It crafts clarity from complexity, opening new possibilities for interpretation and application.

Luwak's strength lies not just in its transformative ability, but in its seamless integration with Aistronaut. Together, they offer a comprehensive, user-friendly, and impactful solution, driving forward ZENTA's mission of innovative excellence in AI-driven healthcare solutions.

Luwak and Aistronaut – turning data into insights, and insights into action.
